Fishing license on your smartphone - How does it work?

1. Search for the waters as you wish or choose a water from the map.

2. View information about the water.

3. Choose duration, and complete the purchase.

4.Your fishing license is now downloaded to your phone.

Good luck, catch a big one!

Purchase of fishing license

Previously it has been difficult to get oriented about where to buy a fishing license, and for which waters it will regard. With Norwegian fishing license for your smartphone (Fiskekort på mobil) it will now be possible to buy the fishing license with ease, regardless if you’re an experienced fisher, a newbie, or just fishing casually with your friends or family.

Quick overview over waters and fishing zones

“Fiskekort på mobil” gives you brief information about waters and zones in the area through the search and map function. For each and every fishing water it will be easy to get an overview over which fishing license is required.

Accessible for everybody

Not everyone are capable to access all the waters around in Norway. By convenience “Fiskekort på mobil” will show accessible fishing areas and waters in which people with disabilities can access. They’re marked with an icon in the search and map function such that everyone will find a place nearby that suits them.

Explore the waters that satisfy your demands

Each water has its restrictions, policies and regulations. Some waters have restrictions on when you can fish and what sort of fish you may fish. With our applications search function, you can easily filter away waters that do not meet your demands. You will also be well informed about policies regarding the waters you decide to fish in.
