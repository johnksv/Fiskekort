For Landlords

Making money from your waters has never been this easy

Make your waters visible

As a landlord you may want to ensure the greatest possible profit from your land and waters. If you publish your waters through “Fiskekort på mobil”, all our users will have instant access to view your waters and buy a fishing license.

Favorable

By disclose information about your waters it will make them more attractive for the users. This may contribute to increased revenue. “Fiskekort på mobil” only takes 5% of the revenue per license purchased. This will be to cover our expenses regarding the service. Favorable for us, favorable for you.

How to publish you waters and zones

In order to publish the waters and zones, you need to access our web portal for landlords*. From the web portal you may fill out information about your waters and zones. You may also set prices, provide pictures, and more. PS: You must prove that you own the land before you can publish your waters and zones. Contact the administration in your municipality if you have question regarding this.

Improve tourism

As a landlord you will get access to data related to your area through the web portal. The data is useful if you want to manage and improve your tourism, because you can review at which time of the year people buyed their fishing license in your area.

*The web portal is still under development and will be available by February 2016.
