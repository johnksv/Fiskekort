Frequently asked questions

Why would I need a fishing license at all?

In Norway it is obligatory to have a fishing license when fishing. The license is issued by the landlord or communes and has previously been available for vending at gas stations and kiosks.

More information regarding the need of a fishing license can be found on these pages. (All of them are in Norwegian):

Laws:

https://lovdata.no/dokument/NL/lov/1992-05-15-47 Chapter IV and chapter V.

https://lovdata.no/dokument/NL/lov/1981-05-29-38/ §31

Regulations:

https://lovdata.no/dokument/SF/forskrift/2007-08-20-987 §5-§7

Can I receive a receipt of the purchase?

Yes. After the purchase is confirmed you can choose to receive a receipt as an email, or to your phone via SMS.

May I use the “Fiskekort på mobil” application on my smartwatch?

Soon. We are currently working on a solution so you can display your purchased fishing license on your smartwatch. This will make it easier for you to show the license upon request by a controller, and you do not need to worry about losing your phone in the water.

What data will the app collect?

Anonymous data regarding a purchase. You may read more about this in the privacy section.Link to privacy.

Why does the app ask for access to my position?

To better give suggestions for nearby waters and zones. You can turn on or off the access from the settings. You may read more about this in the privacy section. Link to privacy.

Will I be notified if I enter a fishing zone that I do not have a valid license for?

Yes, but only if the position settings are enabled. In order to get notifications it is necessary that the application is open on your phone.

May I change the background map?

Yes. In the settings you may choose between multiple background maps. As default the map from “kartverket” is chosen.

May I change the measurement units? (Miles and kilometers)

You may change the measurement units in the settings. There you can choose what unit system you want to use..

May I buy a fishing license for multiple persons?

Yes. You can choose how many fishing licenses you want to buy at checkout.
