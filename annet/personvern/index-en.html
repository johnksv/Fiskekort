Privacy

Your privacy in focus!

We have your privacy in mind - due to the fact that we only collect anonymous data. The information we collect regards the date of purchase, sort of license purchased and what zone it is valid for. The data collected will be used for statistics by the landlords and “Fiskekort på mobil”.

Terms and conditions regarding the application

The application requires an active internet connection. While installing you will get a notification that we may use your position. The position is only used if activated by the user in the settings. The position will only be used to give you suggestions for waters and zones in your area. The app also offer the feature of informing you whenever you enter a new zone. Position data is never saved.

What data will we collect?

Date of when the fishing license was purchased.

What sort of fishing license that was purchased (daily, weekly or seasonal).

Which waters or zone the fishing license is valid for.

How is the purchase handled?

The payment process is handled by an external payment service. We have not yet come to an agreement with any services. Information about the purchase is handled will therefore not come before this is settled
